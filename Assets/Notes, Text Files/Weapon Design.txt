Weapons
Currently thinking about 3 tiers of weapons, with 3-4 weapons each.

Small Arms: Weapons that can be carried by a real-life human. Primaries you use in COD, Battlefield, can buy at the gun store

Rifle: 30 rounds/mag. 750 rounds/minute (2.5/sec). 3 second reload. 25 damage per hit. Hitscan.
Shotgun: 5 round capacity. 1 shot/second. ~1.5 second reload per shell, can stop reloading before full. 
Shoots 11 pellets in a random spread cone, one pellet always dead center, 8 damage/pellet. Hitscan.
Submachine gun: 40 rounds/mag. 1800 rounds/minute (6/sec). 4 second reload. 15 damage per hit. Hitscan.


Big Arms: Weapons that are used by the military. Not necessarily man-portable, but they exist or could exist

Rocket launcher
Railgun (piercing)
